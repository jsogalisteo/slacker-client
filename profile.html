Jorge joined Eckler’s Communication practice in October of 2016 as a graphic designer. At Eckler, he works on a wide range of print and digital communication materials in the areas of health and group benefits for clients in the private and public sectors.

He holds a Master’s Degree in Graphic Design from Elisava School of Design and Engineering (Barcelona, Spain), as well as a Licentiate’s Degree in Journalism from the Autonomous University of Barcelona. Before joining Eckler, Jorge worked as a designer for a variety of agencies and media/publishing companies in Australia, Canada and Ireland.
